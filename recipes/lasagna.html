Classic Cheese Lasagna

This deliciously classic cheese lasagna is perfect for the whole family. Add these simple ingredients to your grocery list and make it today!

Ingredients

  • Mix ricotta
  • 3/4 of the shredded mozzarella
  • parmesan
  • parsley
  • basil
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Spreasd 1 cup of sauce in a 13x9" baking pan
  2. Place 3 uncooked lasagna sheets at the bottom of the pan
  3. Add a layer of 1/4 of the cheese mixture
  4. Add a layer with 1 cup of sauce
  5. Place 3 lasagna sheets and repeat layers until all of the cheese mixture is gone, with the last layer being sauce
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour
